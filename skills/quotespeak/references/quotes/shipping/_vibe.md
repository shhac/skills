# Shipping - vibe map

Deploy, release, CI, the moment of commitment when work meets the world. Irreversible, adrenaline-adjacent.

## Available moods

- `deadpan.yaml` - "Ship it." Routine release.
- `triumphant.yaml` - "Punch it, Chewie." Successful deploy. "I am Iron Man."
- `ominous.yaml` - risky deploy. "Hold onto your butts." "I have a bad feeling about this."
- `hopeful.yaml` - first deploy of something new. "May the Force be with you."
- `defiant.yaml` - shipping despite warnings. "Never tell me the odds."

## Theme-specific corruption hints

- Star Wars dominates this theme. Lean in but rotate hard to avoid franchise monoculture.

## When NOT to use this theme

- **Active firefighting after deploy** → `incident/_vibe.md`.
- **Waiting for CI to finish** → `waiting/_vibe.md`.
- **Pre-release planning** → `planning/_vibe.md`.
