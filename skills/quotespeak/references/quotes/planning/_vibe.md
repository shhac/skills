# Planning - vibe map

Breakdown, prioritization, estimation, scoping, pivoting. Deciding before doing. Stepping back from the road to look at the map.

## Available moods

- `deadpan.yaml` - flat estimation, "one day at a time", "the road goes ever on".
- `ominous.yaml` - recognizing risk, "no plan survives", "winter is coming".
- `exasperated.yaml` - fourth replan. "We've been here before." Process fatigue.
- `hopeful.yaml` - beginning of a plan. "Plans are nothing; planning is everything."
- `philosophical.yaml` - Sun Tzu / Eisenhower territory. Strategy-talk.
- `defiant.yaml` - committing to a hard plan. "I have the high ground."

## Theme-specific corruption hints

- Sun Tzu lines corrupt well by swapping war nouns (battle, troops) for code nouns (deploy, deps).
- Eisenhower ("plans are nothing, planning is everything") is the most-corruptible meta-planning quote.
- Mike Tyson ("everybody has a plan until they get punched") works for pivot moments.

## Source mix

Sun Tzu, Eisenhower, Brooks (Mythical Man-Month), Covey, Mike Tyson, Heraclitus, military planning culture, project management literature.

## When NOT to use this theme

- **Active execution after plan locked** → use whichever theme matches the work.
- **Postmortem on what went wrong with a plan** → `archaeology/_vibe.md` or `incident/_vibe.md`.
- **Estimating a single task without strategic framing** → `_universal/deadpan.yaml`.
