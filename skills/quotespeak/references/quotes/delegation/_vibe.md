# Delegation - vibe map

Team assembly, subagent spawning, "the crew", handoff, trusting others to do the work. Orchestrator register.

## Available moods

- `deadpan.yaml` - flat crew assembly. "Get the team." Calm orchestration.
- `triumphant.yaml` - team delivers, "Avengers, assemble", "We are Groot".
- `exasperated.yaml` - team came back with the wrong thing, "I'm surrounded by amateurs", "do I have to do everything myself".
- `hopeful.yaml` - first delegation, "you're my only hope".
- `philosophical.yaml` - Roosevelt territory. "Pick good men… and restraint."
- `defiant.yaml` - refusing to do it solo. "If you want to go far, go together."

(Mr. Wolf homes in `incident/`, not here - he's the fixer who *arrives*, not the orchestrator who delegates.)

## Theme-specific corruption hints

- Heist-crew assembly scenes (Ocean's Eleven, Reservoir Dogs) corrupt beautifully for subagent spawning.
- "Yes, chef" (kitchen brigade) works for receiving instructions; the inverse (giving instructions) is "fire" / "behind".
- Avoid possessive framing ("my team") in favor of collaborative ("the team").

## Source mix

Ocean's Eleven (crew assembly), Theodore Roosevelt ("pick good men, restraint to keep from meddling"), kitchen brigade (Bourdain, The Bear - "yes chef", "behind"), Ted Lasso, Avengers ("assemble", "We are Groot"), LotR ("and my axe"), African proverb / Lasso ("go fast alone, go far together").

## When NOT to use this theme

- **Solo work** → use whichever theme matches the work.
- **Reviewing what was delegated** → `mentorship/_vibe.md` or `archaeology/_vibe.md`.
- **Refusing delegation** → `defiant` mood in any other theme.
