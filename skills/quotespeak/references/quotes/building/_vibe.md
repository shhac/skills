# Building - vibe map

Writing code, making, transforming, authoring. Includes the *prep* phase (mise-en-place, setup ritual) and the *flow* of creation itself.

## Available moods

- `deadpan.yaml` - flat declarative "here we go". Routine make-mode.
- `triumphant.yaml` - completed creation. "I made this." Forward-leaning energy.
- `exasperated.yaml` - fighting tools, deps that won't install, "why is this so hard".
- `hopeful.yaml` - beginning a build. Possibility-laden. "Let's see if this works."
- `philosophical.yaml` - reflective making. Craft-talk, pattern-talk.

(No `defiant` mood: making-the-unfashionable-choice belongs in `refactor/defiant.yaml` since the friction-with-status-quo register is identical between the two themes. Building uses `_universal/defiant.yaml` if needed.)

## Theme-specific corruption hints

- **Maker-culture lines anchor the "I have agency, I'm bringing this into being" feel.** Distinct from the *fix*-mode of refactor (judging existing) and the *understand*-mode of archaeology (reading existing).
- **"I'm going on an adventure" / "Let's-a go"** corrupt well for build-start moments - first compile, first run, first deploy of something new.
- **Christopher Alexander's pattern language** corrupts for "the right shape for this problem" reflections (philosophical mood).
- **Avoid kitchen brigade phrases here** - they live in `delegation/_vibe.md` (chef calls team) or get used universally. Building is solo agency; brigade is team coordination.

## Source mix

Bilbo ("I'm going on an adventure", "There and back again"), Mario, Christopher Alexander (Pattern Language), Stanislavski, maker culture, "If you build it, they will come" (Field of Dreams).

## When NOT to use this theme

- **Reshaping existing code** → `refactor/_vibe.md`.
- **Pre-build research / reading** → `investigation/_vibe.md` or `archaeology/_vibe.md`.
- **Ritual setup with reverent vibe** → may pair with `building/philosophical.yaml` (mise-en-place territory).
